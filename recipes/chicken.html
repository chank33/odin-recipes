Low kcal, high protein, maxiumum taste Chicken

Description

This recipe is a blend of minimal spices with maximum effect.

Only a few ingredients are required to create this culinary masterpiece

For a full list, please see below..:

BROAT Chicken ingredients

You will need:

  • Chicken thigh. 600g.
  • Mushroom. 200g.
  • Chicken stock. 1 Cup
  • Brown or Red Onion. 300g 1 or Large
  • MSG. 5-10g
  • Salt. To taste
  • Pepper. To taste
Optional, depending on the mood
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Coriander
  • Cinnamon

Or

  • Knorrs Aromat

Steps of creation!

  1. Add Chicken + stock to slow cooker
  2. Bring pan to high heat
  3. Chop Onion
  4. Cook until soft / brown
  5. Add Onion to slow cooker
  6. Add spices, salt and pepper to slow cooker
  7. Cook on low for 5-7 hours

Optional bonus step

Prepare 100g of dry rice to enjoy with the Chicken creation.
