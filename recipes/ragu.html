Low kcal, high protein, maxiumum taste Beef Ragu

Description

This recipe is a blend of minimal spices with maximum effect.

Only a few ingredients are required to create this culinary masterpiece

For a full list, please see below..:

BROAT Ragu ingredients

You will need:

  • Ground beef. 500g
  • Chopped/Tinned Tomatos. 1 Can / 240g
  • Brown or Red Onion. 300g 1 or Large
  • MSG. 5-10g
  • Salt. To taste
  • Pepper. To taste
Optional, depending on the mood
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Coriander
  • Cinnamon

Or

  • Knorrs Aromat

Steps of creation!

  1. Bring pan to high heat
  2. Chop Onion
  3. Cook until soft / brown
  4. Add 500g of ground beef
  5. Add all spices / MSG right before browning, when the beef fat enters the pan
  6. Once meat is brown, add tinned tomatoes
  7. Turn to low/medium-low heat, simmer for 60 mins or until desired consistency

Optional bonus step

Cook pasta with 20 mins or so left on the simmer, then add the starchy pasta sauce to the meat pan to create a nice thick pan sauce.
